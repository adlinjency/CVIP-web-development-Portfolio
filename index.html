I designed a Logo for a beauty product startup Stefrin Naturalz. It is an organic based handmade beauty product business. The logo was to symbolize the purity and naturality of the product which will help to consumers to emphasize their natural beauty. The pink color in the logo was used to symbolise the love for others with which the product was made. The leaf was used to symbolise the organic quality of the product. Overall the logo was to convey the consumers that the product was carefully handmade with with all the love, care and affection by a seller to their customer.
